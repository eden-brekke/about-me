Life Goals

My main and Ultimate goal in life is to be happy!

On top of that a big goal for me is the proud of what I have accomplished and the work that I do. In terms of a career I want to learn these software developer skills and apply it back to my career in virology.
I didn't leave science because I was unhappy, but instead that I wanted to learn more skills to help the field.

During my time in the lab we had a very kind developer write some simple code that allowed us to analyze viral RNA structure and how changing individual nucleotides contributed to the pathogenesis of that virus.
Through cloning techniques we were able to show that just a SiNGLE nucleotide change in the 3'UTR (3 prime Untranslated region) drastically changed the pathology of VEEV.

Seeing just how easy this very simple code made this process, that before took us days to do, really sparked my motivation to seek out these new skills. I'm very excited to pursue this field and to make connections with my peers.
